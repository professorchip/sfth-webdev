Straight From The Heart

Counseling for individuals, couples and families

Straight From the Heart provides quality outpatient mental health services in a trauma informed environment. It is our hope that the therapeutic relationship we engender will foster a safe space conducive to healing and emotional growth.

  • Melissa Belsink, LCSW
